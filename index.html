A missing game recommender for Steam users

A missing game recommender for Steam users who have suffered a long painful way to find a new and awesome game, in the flood of biased and exaggerated comments and recommendations online.

I am a long-term Steam user, who has devoted hundreds of hours to have fun with my friends on various games. However, there is one thing that confuses me the most. Whenever I try to get a new game, I usually ends up buying the one that I immediately lose my interest. Every game looks fantastic, if you only have a grasp on their trailer or reviews done by "Big Website". It is so biased and not trust-worthy. That's why I try to build a simple yet powerful recommender, to at least ease my pain and it works pretty well now.

It is built upon Django framework, Postgres database and an information-retrieval recommender. It is deployed on Heroku. For more info, you can check on my github page.

Get excited? Try it now: http://gamesyoumiss.herokuapp.com/recom/
